Beneath the frozen isles, a hidden flame waits to burn.
When fifteen-year-old Ash Valtor shatters ancient laws with forbidden fire, he awakens the wrath of a tyrant empire—and the shadowed legacy of his family’s past. As old powers rise and the world cracks with revolution, Ash must choose: surrender to darkness, or become the spark that reignites hope.

Elderstone is a sweeping epic of magic, survival, and rebellion in a world where every ember is a risk—and every secret, a weapon.
